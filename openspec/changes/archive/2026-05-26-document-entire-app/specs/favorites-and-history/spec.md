## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: favorites-list
The system SHALL allow users to bookmark or "favorite" products and recipes for quick access later.

#### Scenario: Liking an item
- **WHEN** the favorite button is toggled
- **THEN** the item is added to the favorites list

### Requirement: full-scan-history
The system SHALL maintain a chronological log of every product scanned by the user, providing a UI to review past scans.

#### Scenario: Viewing history
- **WHEN** the history tab is opened
- **THEN** a chronological list of past scans is displayed
