## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: account-management
The system SHALL provide a Customer Center for users to view data usage, read the privacy policy and terms and conditions.

#### Scenario: Viewing policies
- **WHEN** the user navigates to settings
- **THEN** they can read the privacy policy

### Requirement: account-deletion
The system SHALL provide a secure flow for users to completely delete their account and wipe all associated data from the database in compliance with privacy regulations.

#### Scenario: Deleting account
- **WHEN** the account deletion is confirmed
- **THEN** all user data is removed from the system
